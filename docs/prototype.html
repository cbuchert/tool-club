Foundry tour — Provo Iron Works

Ryan H. · Host: Dana B. · Votes close Apr 20
11
Provo Iron Works has been casting metal parts for local industries since 1948. I reached out to the owner — a friend of a friend — and he's willing to do a 90-minute tour of the full foundry floor. We'd see pattern making, molding, melting, and pour.

Probably cap at 12 people for safety. Hearing protection required. Steel-toed boots recommended but not required.

Woad dyeing

Dana B. · Host: Dana B. · Voting closed
▲
14
This suggestion became an event.
Dana proposed a natural dye session using woad she'd grown in her back garden. The idea was to process fresh leaves, set up vat dye baths, and dye small fabric swatches to take home. No experience needed — the whole process takes about 2 hours.
